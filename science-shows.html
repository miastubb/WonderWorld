Our Science Shows

Cryogenics / Liquid Nitrogen Show

Our scientist uses super-cold liquid nitrogen (-196°C / -321°F) to instantly freeze everyday objects.

Demo Highlights

Shattering Flowers & Rubber Tubing:A rose or a banana becomes brittle as glass and shatters when hit. Cloud Chamber: Pouring LN₂ on the floor creates a rolling, spooky cloud effect Ice Cream Instant: Making ice cream on the spot by freezing the ingredients in a metal bowl. Overinflated Balloon: A fully inflated balloon is dipped in LN₂ and shrivels to a tiny size, then re-inflates as it warms up.

IGNITION! The Science Of Fire And Fury

"IGNITION!" is a high-energy, 25-minute live demonstration show that explores the exciting and often misunderstood world of combustion chemistry.

We don't just show things burning—we deconstruct the classic "fire triangle" (fuel, heat, oxygen) through a series of breathtaking, large-scale experiments that are as educational as they are unforgettable.

This show transforms the abstract concepts of oxidation, exothermic reactions, and energy states into a visceral, awe-inspiring experience.

Fire & Color!

Our 'Fire & Color' demo show turns chemistry into an unforgettable spectacle. We don't just explain science—we make it visceral.

We'll showcase vibrant rainbow flames from simple salts, revealing the unique fingerprint of each element. It's safe, visually stunning, and directly tied to curriculum standards on chemical reactions and energy.
