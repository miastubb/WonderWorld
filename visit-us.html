Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair ramps, audio guides, braille signs, and elevators. Everyone should feel welcome at WonderWorld.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, coffee, snacks, and full meals.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia, gifts, activity sets, books, and games, so you can bring science home with you.
